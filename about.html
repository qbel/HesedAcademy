Hesed Academy operates with a learner-centered philosophy of education, i.e., our students’ overall best interest always remains the primary motivation for what we do. We are guided by our Christian values and professional ethics. And, we adhere to the local education regulations and corporate business laws. Hesed Academy is wholly owned by Hesed Resource & Development Foundation (HESED RDF), a non-profit faith-based corporation that is focused on human capital development and societal transformation.

Vision:



We envision Hesed Academy as a leading Christian educational center of excellence for raising competent spirit-filled transformational servant leaders for the diverse fields of human endeavor for God’s glory and human good.



Mission:


The mission of Hesed Academy is to provide a Christ-honoring conducive environment and community that facilitates learning in Science, Technology, Engineering, Arts, and Mathematics that prepares young people for life-long commitments to learning, professional excellence, moral virtue, discipleship, and servant leadership in all areas of life.
